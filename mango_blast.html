Mango Blast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Mango
  • 2 tbsp Monkfruit Sweetener
  • 3 spoons Yogurt
  • ½ cup Milk

Instructions

  1. Cut the mango along the seed.
  2. Scoop out mango into blender.
  3. Put in the Monkfruit Sweetener.
  4. Pour in the Milk
  5. Put in the Yogurt
  6. Blend in blender and put aside.
  7. Cut all remaing fruit off of the seed and put in ziplock bag.
  8. Mash mango with hand until some chunks remain.
  9. Take popsicle mold and pour in a little bit of smoothie, then scoop in a little bit of the mashed mango. Alternate between the two until full.
  10. Lastly cover mold and freeze for 8-10 hours.
