Banana bread recipe

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup Monkfruit sweetener
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup pecans
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips
  • 1½ cups flour

Instructions

  1. Combine the Monkfruit sweetener and melted butter. Stir.
  2. Preaheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Prepare a loaf pan with butter.
  4. Add the bananas to the mixture and mash until smooth.
  5. Add the egg and vanilla extract. Stir.
  6. Mix in the baking soda, cinnamon, pecans, half of the chocolate chips and flour.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  8. B ake for about 55 minutes then take the bread out. Then let it cool. Coat with chocolate chips
